Characters and Further

The World of Syfy's "The Magicians"

"It didn’t matter where you were, if you were in a room full of books you were at least halfway home." -Lev Grossman, "The Magician's Land"

This site is a fansite dedicated to characters from the Syfy television series "The Magicians" based on book series with the same name written by Lev Grossman. A cornucopia of dark fantasy and action adventure, starring nervous philosophy student Quentin "Q" Coldwater. When he stumbles through a portal that takes him to the entrance of Brakebills University, he is unaware that his life is about to change. Once guided to an entrance exam by flirtatious partier and 3rd year student, Elliot Waugh, Q learns that Brakebills is a University that offers a graduate program for people who show potential for the extraordinary. In short: Magicians. This series follows Q and the friends, allies, rivals, and foes that follow Q through his journey at Brakebills. Whether it be a homicidal magical Beast from the previously thought fictional land of Fillory, angry and borderline abusive study-abroad teachers, or the relationships between all the students themselves, Q and his friends will not have an easy time surviving to graduation. After all, if magic exists, what else does?
